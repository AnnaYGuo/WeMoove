\begin{abstract}
Dance is a universal form of expression and entertainment, yet mastering its intricacies
 can be daunting for beginners. To democratize dance learning and make it more accessible
to enthusiasts, we propose a novel approach utilizing computer vision techniques. By
breaking down dance videos into vectorized and text-based representations with counts,
the complexities of dance routines are simplified, enabling easier comprehension and
replication by average fans.
\end{abstract}
